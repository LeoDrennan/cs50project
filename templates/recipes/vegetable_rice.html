Vegetable Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 Olive Oil
  • 1x Onion
  • 2x Garlic Clove
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Carrots
  • 1 Red Pepper
  • 1 Head of Broccoli
  • Frozen Peas
  • 1Tbsp Grated Ginger
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • 2 Cups Cooked Brown Rice
  • 1 Cup Spinach
  • 3 Green Onions
  • 1Tbsp Soy Sauce

Method

  1. Heat the oil in the wok, add the eggs and scramble. Remove from the wok and add to a bowl.
  2. Add another Tbsp of oil before adding the onions and carrots. (3-5mins)
  3. Add all remaining veggies of choice to the wok. Continue to cook until all veg is golden (3-5mins)
  4. Place the cooked veggies in the same bowl as the scrambled eggs.
  5. Add the last of the oil, and then add the ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes, cook for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  6. Add the rice and mix thoroughly, continue heating. (5mins)
  7. Add the spinach and green onions. Reintroduce the egg and cooked veg back to the wok and stir to combine.
  8. Remove from the heat and add the soy sauce.
