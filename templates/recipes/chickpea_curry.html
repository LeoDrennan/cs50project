Chickpea and Spinach Curry

Ingredients

  • 2Tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1x Onion
  • 2x Garlic Clove
  • 1inch Fresh Ginger
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Curry Powder
  • 8 oz. Spinach
  • Tinned Tomatoes (1 Tin)
  • Tinned Chickpeas (2 Tins)

Method

  1. Dice the onion, mince the garlic, and grate the ginger before sauteing together with the Olive Oil. (5 mins)
  2. Add the curry powder and continue to saute. (1 min)
  3. Add 1/4 cup of water to the pan along with the spinach and saute until wilted. (2 mins)
  4. Drain and rinse the chickpeas before adding to the pan along with the tinned tomatoes.
  5. Stir well, bring to a simmer and cook for 5 minutes.
