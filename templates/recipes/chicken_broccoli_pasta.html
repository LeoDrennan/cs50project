Chicken, Broccoli, and Kale Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2x Chicken Breast
  • 1x Onion
  • 2x Garlic Clove
  • 8 Baby Chestnut Mushrooms
  • 75g Kale
  • 1 Head of Broccoli
  • 600ml Chicken Stock
  • 200g Pasta
  • 100ml Milk
  • 60ml White Wine

Method

  1. Heat the oil over a medium-high heat until up to temperature and then add the two diced chicken breasts. Fry until browned before removing from the pan. (5 mins)
  2. Add a small amount of additional oil before sweating the onions on a medium-low heat.
  3. Add the 2 cloves of crushed garlic, and continue sweating. (2 mins)
  4. Add the mushrooms. (2 mins)
  5. Add the wine and bring to the boil. (5 mins)
  6. Add the chicken stock and the milk. Bring back to the boil before adding the pasta and cooking for 5 minutes.
  7. Add the chicken into the mix. (3 mins)
  8. Add the broccoli. (2 mins)
  9. Add the kale. (5 mins)
  10. Rest for 5 minutes off the heat with the lid on. Add 2 tsp cornflour if required.
