Chicken and Chorizo Jambalaya

Ingredients

  • 2x Chicken Breast
  • 1x Onion
  • 2x Garlic Clove
  • 1x Red Pepper
  • 75g Chorizo
  • Quarter bag of frozen peas
  • 1tbsp Cajun Spice
  • 250g Brown Rice
  • 1x Tin Tomatoes
  • 350ml Chicken Stock

Method

  1. Heat the oil over a medium-high heat until up to temperature and then add the two diced chicken breasts. Add a dusting of cajun spice to coat the meat, and fry until browned before removing from the pan. (5 mins)
  2. Add a small amount of additional oil before sweating the onions on a medium-low heat.
  3. Add the red pepper, 2 cloves of crushed garlic, chorizo, and the tablespoon of cajun spice. (5 mins)
  4. Add the tin of tomatoes, 350ml of chicken stock, and the precooked rice.
  5. Bring to the boil and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the chicken back in before cooking for a further 9 minutes.
  7. Add the peas and cook for a further minute.
  8. Rest for 5 minutes off the heat with the lid on.
